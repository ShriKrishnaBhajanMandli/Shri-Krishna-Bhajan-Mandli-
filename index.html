About Us

Shri Krishna Bhajan Mandli is a devotional group dedicated to spreading the message of love, faith, and devotion through soulful Bhajans and Kirtans. We organize spiritual programs such as Bhajan Sandhya, Kirtan & Satsang, Mata Ki Chowki, Shivcharcha, Awasani Mata Pooja, Sundarkand and other religious gatherings across Vasai, Virar, Nalasopara and nearby areas in Maharashtra.

Our mission is to bring communities together in devotion, creating moments of peace, positivity, and spiritual connection.

Our Service Areas

We proudly organize devotional programs and events in the following areas:

  • Vasai
  • Virar
  • Nalasopara
  • Nearby areas in Palghar & Thane District
