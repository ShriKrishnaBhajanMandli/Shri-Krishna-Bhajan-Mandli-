About Us

Shri Krishna Bhajan Mandli is a devotional group based in Vasai Nalasopara, Maharashtra. We bring people closer to the divine through soulful bhajans, kirtans, satsangs, and spiritual gatherings. With melodious voices and heartfelt devotion, we create a divine atmosphere for temples, homes, and events across India.
